大模型的核心运行机制：基于深度学习，尤其是Transformer架构。其核心是自注意力机制，能够捕捉输入序列中不同部分的关系。模型通过预训练在大规模数据上学习语言规律，再通过微调适应特定任务。训练过程中，使用反向传播和优化算法（Adam）调整数百万甚至数十亿的参数。依赖GPU/CPU等高性能硬件和分步式训练加速计算。输入文本经过分词与嵌入转换为向量标识，模型通过推理生成输出，并采用生成策略确保输出质量。

Core Operating Mechanism of Large Language Models:
Based on deep learning, particularly the Transformer architecture. Its core lies in the self-attention mechanism, which captures relationships between different parts of the input sequence. The model learns linguistic patterns from large-scale data through pre-training, then adapts to specific tasks via fine-tuning. During training, backpropagation and optimization algorithms (such as Adam) are used to adjust millions or even billions of parameters. It relies on high-performance hardware like GPUs/CPUs and distributed training to accelerate computation. Input text is converted into vector representations through tokenization and embedding. The model generates outputs via inference, and applies generation strategies to ensure output quality.
