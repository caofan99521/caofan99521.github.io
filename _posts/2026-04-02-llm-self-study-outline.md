大语言模型（LLM）自学梳理

一、什么是大语言模型？

大语言模型（Large Language Model，简称LLM）是基于Transformer 架构，通过海量文本数据预训练得到的生成式AI,核心能力是理解人类语言、生成自然文本、完成对话、推理等任务。

二、LLM的分类

2.1 自然语言处理模型
专注于文本生成、理解、翻译等任务，GPT系列（Open AI)、BERT（Google）、T5（Google）
AIGC

2.2 计算机视觉（CV）模型
视觉大模型（Large Visual Models）核心是通过大模型数据和复杂模型架构，实现对图像和视频的深度理解和生成。与传统计算机视觉模型相比，视觉大模型具有更强的泛化嗯那你和多任务适应性，能够处理复杂的视觉任务，如图像分类、目标检测、语义分割、图像生成等。

2.3语言模型
语言大模型是基于深度学习技术构建的人工智能模型，主要用于处理语言相关的任务，如语言识别（ASR）,语音合成（TTS）、语音翻译等。近年来，随着深度学习和大规模数据训练的发展，语音大模型在性能和功能上取得了显著进展，能够支持多语言、多场景的复杂任务。

语音识别、语音合成、语音增强、说话人识别

2.4 多模态模型
是一种能够同时处理多种数据模态（如文本、图像、音频、视频等）的人工智能模型。与传统的单模态模型（如仅处理文本或图像）相比，多模态模型通过整合不同模态的数据，能够提供更全面、更准确和理解和生成能力。这些模型在多个领域展现出强大的应用潜力，例如医疗诊断、自动驾驶、智能助手等。


Self-Study Outline of Large Language Models (LLMs)

I. What is a Large Language Model?
A Large Language Model (LLM) is a generative AI model based on the Transformer architecture, pre-trained on massive amounts of text data. Its core capabilities include understanding human language, generating natural text, completing dialogues, reasoning, and more.

II. Classification of LLMs

2.1 Natural Language Processing Models
These models focus on tasks such as text generation, comprehension, and translation. Examples include the GPT series (OpenAI), BERT (Google), and T5 (Google).
(AIGC - AI Generated Content)

2.2 Computer Vision (CV) Models
Large Visual Models are designed to achieve deep understanding and generation of images and videos through large-scale data and complex model architectures. Compared to traditional computer vision models, large visual models offer stronger generalization capabilities and multi-task adaptability, enabling them to handle complex visual tasks such as image classification, object detection, semantic segmentation, and image generation.

2.3 Speech Models
Large speech models are deep learning-based AI models primarily used for speech-related tasks, including Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Text-to-Speech (TTS), and speech translation. In recent years, advancements in deep learning and large-scale data training have significantly improved the performance and functionality of large speech models, allowing them to support complex multilingual and multi-scenario tasks.
(Speech recognition, speech synthesis, speech enhancement, speaker recognition)

2.4 Multimodal Models
These are AI models capable of processing multiple data modalities simultaneously, such as text, images, audio, and video. Compared to traditional unimodal models (e.g., those handling only text or only images), multimodal models integrate different data types to provide more comprehensive and accurate understanding and generation capabilities. These models have shown strong application potential in various fields, including medical diagnosis, autonomous driving, and intelligent assistants.
